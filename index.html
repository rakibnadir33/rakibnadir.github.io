About Me

I'm Rakib Mahmud Nadir, a junior offensive security professional with a strong passion for penetration testing and adversary simulation. I focus on manual enumeration, Active Directory attack chains, and web/network exploitation to identify and demonstrate real-world security weaknesses.

My approach emphasizes realistic threat modeling — from initial reconnaissance through lateral movement to domain compromise — mirroring the tactics used by real-world adversaries. I hold certifications including CRTP, eJPT, CRTA, and CNSP.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Urban and Regional Planning at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), expected December 2027. My cybersecurity journey runs parallel to my academic studies, driven by curiosity and a commitment to ethical hacking.
